How to play

The objective of the game is to find and sink all ten of enemy ships.
There are two types of the game:

  • vs Android - single player game, you play for scores against android device.
  • Internet - multiplayer game, you play against another person.


Ships setup

After selecting the game type, proceed to the ships setup.
There are overall 10 ships of 4 different types:


Aircraft carrier.
Length: four cells
Ships: one

Battleship.
Length: three cells
Ships: two

Destroyer.
Length: two cells
Ships: three

Gunboat.
Length: one cell
Ships: four

Place ships horizontally or vertically by dragging them from the top of the screen. Tap the ship on the field to rotate it. Ships cannot overlap or touch one another.

NOTE:Conflicting cells will be highlighted in red.


You can generate ships automatically by clicking on 'Auto' button. You can later rearrange them.
After setting up all ships, click 'Done' to start the battle!

The Battle

During the gameplay you see your and enemy's fields and the overall status showing the number of ships left from each side. Orange frame indicates whose turn it is now. Shooting is done one cell at a time. If the enemy ship is hit then you are given chance to shoot again. If you miss turn is passed to your opponent.

The game is over when one side has lost all of his/her ships.
